Audiovisual Analysis: Trump On Bilibili

August 2023

Scraping audiovisual data from Bilibili videos to analyze trends in video data and sentiment towards Donald Trump.

Project outline:

  1. Extract video frames and video audio in Python and process with object-oriented programming
  2. For each frame, calculate dominant color, brightness, and entropy
  3. For audios, calculate loudness and BPM
  4. Ongoing: Identify faces and analyze emotions using DeepFace AI
